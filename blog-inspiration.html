Tiki Taka - and Beyond

The Pep Guardiola Way, 28th January 2020

So a large part of my coaching philosophy is to make sure I keep learning from best practice, and then see how I can apply what I've learnt to any player/group/team I am coaching. I do this not only for my own curiosity to to ensure that my coaching remains upd-to-date and innovative. In recent times, I have decided to learn moe about one of the current world's best - Pep Guardiola. Why? Well, his record I think speaks for itself, winning Premier League titles in a row recently in record-breaking style. This is just a few of the many great achievements of the current Manchester City manager, all down to masterful coaching and innovative tactics. In order to begin my understanding of his coaching and tactics, I've recently acquired the two books below (click on the images to find where you can buy them). I can highly recommend these if you're a coach, current or aspriring, and want to try out new ideas.
