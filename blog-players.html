For the Players

Do the basics - excellently

How to raise your game, 15th January 2020

So the the mistake we make as players sometimes is that we think we need to be able to pull off the amazing goals/tricks we see the pros doing on Youtube video montages. And while one can train these too, the real reason pros find themselves at the top level is that they do the do the absolute basics to a world class level. Players like former Barcelona greats Xavi and Iniesta - great examples of doing the basics superbly. Passing, first touch, dribbling with the ball come at ease for these less physical players. The good news is that you can improve on these things absolutely anywhere, all you need is a ball, a wall and/or goal! How much of my time it will take up you ask? 15-30 minutes a day max will start producing incredible results in 3 months already. But it's important we use this time training efficiently, so I've provided below a great video from Unisport showing just exactly what you could train in those 15-30 minutes.
