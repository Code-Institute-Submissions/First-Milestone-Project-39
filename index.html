Training Hard to Training SMART

About Me

I am Gustaf Ljungwaldh, an experienced soccer coach based in Stockholm, Sweden. I have 13 years of diverse coaching experiences of players and teams, ranging from:

  • Ages 6-40
  • Regions of United Kingdom, Sweden, Spain and Taiwan
  • Male and Female
  • Amateur level to Elite Youth level

SMART Philosophy

I have also gathered 5 successful years of professional project management experience. Along this jounrney I have discovered how powerful it can be to use SMART goal setting to achieve anything:

  • S - Is your goal Specific?
  • M - Is your goal Measurable?
  • A - Is your goal Attainable?
  • R - Is your goal Realistic?
  • T - Is your goal Time-Bound?

How I Can Help

No, I'm not a former professional soccer player. However, I went from playing no soccer up until 14 years-old to both playing at a competitive amateur level and coaching by the time I was 16 years-old. How you may ask? By working SMART - working concretely on what I needed to improve on in a structured way. I passed this methodology on to coaching individual players, groups of players and teams, helping them all to achieve their goals and ultimately grow. I can do the same for you!
